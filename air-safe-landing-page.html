Although we don't realise it, the air is the largest surface in any building and must be cleaned, like any other surface in the building. Our AirSafe filters efficiently, quickly, & cost effectively make our air safe.

AirSafe’s ability to capture and hold particles the size of all known airborne pathogens is a true breakthrough in filtration. It greatly improves our ability to contain the spread of all respiratory viruses, such as seasonal cold and flu and airborne bacterial infections

Available for the first time ever to residential clients, our filters scrub sub-micron sized airborne respiratory viruses from the air. After AirSafe has been installed on your fan (which you can buy online from Amazon), the very high efficiency filtration can reduce airborne particles in a room by up to 93% in 3 hours.

The actual time will vary with the power of your fan. But even with a smaller fan which takes longer, the air will still be cleaned. We can give you suggestions for which fans to buy, depending on the size of your rooms.

AirSafe truly is a breakthrough in creating healthy indoor air. It’s like putting a safety mask on your home.

How is AirSafe Different?

AirSafe is a synthetic electrostatic filter that has efficiencies higher than HEPA, 99.9998% @.1 micron. It is a soft flexible pad with a low static pressure. This allows for HEPA or near HEPA filtration of the air, which is the same standard used by hospital operating theatres.

It also means that the air in all your rooms can be filtered without the huge costs of rebuilding your AC units. By attaching an AirSafe filter to a free-standing fan you will capture any microbe that is floating in the air. Just move the fan from room to room to keep the air safe and clean.

AirSafe will change the lives of any immune-compromised patients, oncology patients, infants, and the elderly by drastically reducing the risk that they might come into contact with a dangerous submicron particle.

Why do I need such High Filtration?

You can see on this chart how small virus and bacteria particles are. But in this part of the world, it’s not just the sub-micron particles that are dangerous. Very fine dust, called “permanently suspended particles” are also a danger, because they build up in the lungs. And ultra-fine dust is disturbed very time you wipe a surface, because the dust becomes airborne again for a time. AirSafe captures ALL these airborne contaminants whenever you switch on your fan.
